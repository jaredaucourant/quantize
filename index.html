Hivemind

We've always felt that your mood is an average based on the mood of everyone around you. What does your city sound like? Quantize analyzes twitter data to reveal the collective conscience and creates its soundtrack.

Explore a data visualization of your listening habits, graphed accordingly via the Mood Matrix. Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram integration allows you to share the results.
